About me
I'm Amanda Huang a 19 years old girl from Taiwan. In the future, I would like to be a Product Designer, an Electrical Engineering, or UX/UI Designer. I enjoy using my creativity to solve daily life issues. My goal is to find creative solutions to daily problems falls in line with industrial or product design. I feel confident and satisfied when I hear people say, “your ideas are pretty good” or “I never thought about that.” Empathy is a skill I find more important than ever in the three years of Design Thinking courses I took in high school. The designer is not always the one who is experiencing the problem, so it is important to comprehend the problem in the user's shoes. Everyone has lots of creativity but being able to shine from the rest depends on whether you can make the idea become a reality. Whether you are an Electrical Engineering or Product Designer, the success of a project is what makes you stand out from the crowd. If you have “empathy” in what you are doing, you are already halfway successful in your career.
